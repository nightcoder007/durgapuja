LETS HAVE A LOOK INTO MY LIFE:)

INTRODUCTION

My name is papai debnath.I come from a small villege named barasat in NADIA in WEST BENGAL.I love and proud to be an indian.I am 19 years old and very foody.

NURSERY SCHOOL

IN MY CHILDHOOD I HAD TAKEN MY FIRST PROPER EDUCATION FROM THE TARANGABALA SISU NIKETAN WHICH IS ALSO RUNNING NOW IN MY VILLEGE.THOSE WERE THE BEST DAYS OF MY LIFE WHERE IS NO PRESSURE OF LEARNING ,ME AND MY FRIENDS USED TO PLAY AND ALSO WE DID SOME NOTIOUS ACTS.

PRIMARY SCHOOL

IN THE NEXT STEP TOWARDS MY EDUCATION IS MY PRIMARY SCHOOL WHICH WASRAMKRISHNA PRATAMIK BIDYAPITH.ITS A PLEASURE FOR ME BECAUSE COINCIDENTLY BOTH MY NURSERY AND MY PRIMARY SCHOOL ARE AT A SAME PLACE.SO IT WASNOT A NEW THING FOR ME BUT THE FUN AND PLEASURE IS DOUBLED THIS TIME.BUT ITS VERY HEART-BREAKING TO TELL THAT MY BELOVED PRIMARY SCHOOL IS NO MORE BUT THE GOOD THING IS THAT IN A REPLACE WITH THIS,THERE BUILDED UP A NEW PRIMARY SCHOOL WITH THE SAME NAME.

HIGH SCHOOL

LIKE EVERY OTHERS FOR ME IT WAS A DREAM COMING TRUE TO GO TO A HIGH SCHOOL.I WAS VERY FEARED BUT AT THE SAME TIME I WAS VERY CURIOUS TO MEET SOME NEW FRIENDS AND ALSO TO GO TO A NEW PLACE.MY HIGH SCHOOL NAME ISANJANGARH HIGH SCHOOL.MY SCHOOL IS BESIDE THE RIVERANJANA.THIS 5 YEAR JOURNEY NOT ONLY CHANGED MY LIFE BUT ALSO CHANGED MY WHOLE PERSPECTIVE TO SEE THE WORLD IN ADIFFERENT WAY.THROUGHT THIS 5 YEAR I MADE MANY FRIENDS WITH WHOM I WANNA BE CONNECTED THROUGHT MY LIFE.WITH MY BUDDIES I WENT TO MANY NEARBY PLACES AFTER THE SCHOOL OR IN THE TIFFIN TIME.THERE WERE MANY FAST FOOD STALL NEAR TO MY SCHOOL.WE DID CYCLING A LOT.BUT ONE OF THE BIGGEST UNFULFILLED DESIRE OF MINE DURING THE SCHOOLLIFE IS NOT HAVING ANY GIRLFRIEND.THOUGH MANY OF MY FRIENDS GOT THIS CHANCE FOR ME ITS NOT WORKED WELL.JOKES APART, THE SCHOOLLIFE IS THE MOST PRECIOUS TIME TO BUILD A STRONG BASE TOWARDS MY FUTURE BUT ALSO GIVE ME THE REASON TO LEARN MORE.

AAKASH INSTITUTE

MY WISH WAS TO DO ENGINEERING AFTER MY HIGHER SECONDARY EDUCATION.SO I DECIDED TO PREPARE FOR JEE.FOR THIS REASON I ADMITTED TO AAKASH INSTITUTE IN BARRACKPORE.IN THIS 1 YEAR COURSE I HAD ANOTHER UNFORGOTABLE EXPERIENCE OF TRAIN JOURNEY ON DAILY BASIS.EVERYDAY I HAD TO WAKE UP EARLY IN THE MORNING TO CATCH THE TRAIN FROM TAHERPUR STATION.IT TOOK ALMOST 3 HRS JOURNEY BY TRAIN SO IN THIS TIME PERIOD EVERYDAY I CAME TO KNOW DIFFERENT FACTS ,RULES,SYSTEM AND ALSO INTERECTED WITH DIFFERENT PEOPLE.THERE IS SOME BAD MEMORY WHERE SOME QUERELL HAPPENED BUT A LOT OF GREAT MEMEORIES OVERWRITE THE WORSE ONES.I MADE MORE NEW FRIENDS THERE AND ALSO MANY OF MY SCHOOL FRIENDS WERE THERE.SO IT WAS A NICE JOURNEY WITH FULL OF HAPPINESS AND ALSOTHE SORROW TO LOSE ME NEW PHONE[STOLEN BY THIEF](:.

COLLEGE

AFTER COMPLETION OF MY COURSE THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC STARTED TO TREAT THE ENTIRE WORLD SO FOR MINE COLLEGE JOURNEY TO START I HAD TO WAIT FOR MORE 6 MONTHS.BUT THE WAIT ENDED UP WHEN I GOT A CHANCE TO GET ADMISSION IN GOVERNMENT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TEXTILE TECHNOLOGY,BERHAMPORE. I AM PARSUING B.TECH DEGREE IN COMPUTERSCIENCE & ENGINEERING.THOUGH I DIDNT GET A CHANCE TO VISIT THERE AND GET A LECTURE MANUALLY FOR COVID REASONS AND GUIDELINES BUT I AM HOPING THAT THIS SITUATION WILL PASS BY AND WE ALL WILL CAN LIVE FREELY.
